About Me

I'm Omar Dahmouch, a graphic designer passionate about creating bold, meaningful visual experiences. Originally from Tunisia, currently based in Dammam, Saudi Arabia.

With over 4 years of professional experience, I've worked across branding, social media design, and motion graphics for companies in both Tunisia and Saudi Arabia.

I hold a Higher Technician Certificate in Telecommunications (Prepress) from the Graphic Arts Training Center in Ariana, and a Motion Design certificate from Netinfo. Fluent in French, Arabic, and English.
